PHYSICAL SCREENING TEST STANDARDS FOR SEAL AND SWCC

By: Naval Special Warfare
Posted: March 4, 2020


NSW PST STANDARDS - TO QUALIFY FOR A SEAL OR SWCC CONTRACT

To qualify for a SEAL or SWCC contract, and before you ever get to the Naval Special Warfare Preparatory School (NSW PREP), you have to meet the minimum Physical Screening Test (PST) standard. If you fail the PST your dream of becoming a SEAL or SWCC will be over.

ENLISTED SEAL
Exercise Time Rest Average Minimum
Swim 500 yards (breast or sidestroke) Unlimited 10:00 min. 9:30 12:30
Push-up 2:00 min. 2:00 min. 75 50
Curl-up 2:00 min. 2:00 min. 75 50
Pull-up 2:00 min. 10:00 min. 15 10
Run 1.5 miles Unlimited Event over 9:30 10:30
Use the SEAL PST Calculator Find out if you've got what it takes.
