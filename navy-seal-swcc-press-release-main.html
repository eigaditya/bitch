SEALSWCC.COM is an official U.S. Government website. Provided as a public service by Commander, Naval Special Warfare Center. The Naval Special Warfare Assessment Detachment (NSWAD) is the authoritative voice for Naval Special Warfare training and heritage. We are the public affairs community outreach function of the Naval Special Warfare Center. It is our mission to explain the training and selection process, requirements, standards, and accomplishments peculiar to Naval Special Warfare.
