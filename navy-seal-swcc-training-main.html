SEAL OR SWCC SWIM TRAINING

The swim training program is for active duty only. The swim training program for BUD/S or BCS is held in Little Creek, VA. Swim training happens Monday - Friday from 1:00 - 3:00 p.m. Wear a mask when entering or exiting the swim training building. Swim training is conducted by the Naval Special Warfare Assessment Detachment.
